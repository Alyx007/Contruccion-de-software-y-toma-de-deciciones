♥Mi Personaje Favorito♥

Darth Maul is a formidable and iconic character within the Star Wars universe, renowned for his menacing appearance and fearsome combat prowess. Introduced in "Star Wars: Episode I - The Phantom Menace," Maul is a Sith Lord trained by Darth Sidious, the mastermind behind the Galactic Empire. With his striking red and black facial tattoos and double-bladed lightsaber, Maul quickly became a memorable villain. Despite his limited screen time in the films, Maul's presence left a lasting impact on fans, leading to his expanded portrayal in animated series and other media. His complex character arc, which includes survival against seemingly insurmountable odds and seeking vengeance against his former master, further adds depth to his villainous persona, solidifying his status as one of the most iconic antagonists in the Star Wars saga
